{"type": "text", "content": "Much prior work has studied generative modeling of video data using a variety of methods, including recurrent networks, generative adversarial networks, autoregressive transformers, and diffusion models. These works often focus on a narrow category of visual data, on shorter videos, or on videos of a fixed size. Sora is a generalist model of visual data\u2014it can generate videos and images spanning diverse durations, aspect ratios and resolutions, up to a full minute of high definition video."}
